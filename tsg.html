Who are we?

Travelling is one of life's great luxuries. However, travelling is not always the safest, especially for women, who face challenges whilst travelling, such as:

  • Travelling at night
  • Using public transport
  • Unwanted attention
....And so much more. A 2019 survey by the ABTA Travel Association found that 84 percent of all solo travelers are women. As a result, it is vital to ensure, that all female travellers, whether solo or not, have the resources they need to feel safe. Our aim, therefore, is to help make travelling safe for everyone.
